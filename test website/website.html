For this project there was a clear state diagram that needed to be followed. The video starts with showing that our internal clock is at 8 seconds and is counting down. Once 0 seconds is reached we will change states from our north and south bound lights going from a state of green to yellow to red. While the east and west bound lights need to change state from the current red to green. The north and south lights will hold a state of yellow for 6 seconds before swithcing over to red. While the east and west lights will continue to hold a state of red untill the north and south bound lights are in the red state for a full 2 seconds. Then the east and west bound lights can change to green as shown in the video and state diagram.
